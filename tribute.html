About Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel

Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel was an Indian barrister, politician, and one of the founding fathers of the Republic of India. He played a crucial role in the Indian independence movement and was instrumental in the integration of princely states into a unified India.

As the first Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Home Affairs of India, he led the task of uniting the country's 565 princely states into a single nation. He is often referred to as the "Iron Man of India" for his firm leadership and commitment to the integrity of the nation.

Patel's contributions to India's unity and integration are widely recognized and he is considered one of the most influential leaders in Indian history.
